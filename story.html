My Story

I started streaming because I wanted family and friends to feel like they were at the rink, even when life kept them away. I am not a business and I do not do this for money. It is a passion project that helps kids share their moments and helps me push everyday tech to its limits.

First stream

One camera, a laptop, and a lot of trial and error. The goal was simple: share a game with grandparents and teammates who could not make it.

  • Keep it easy and press go.
  • Focus on the story, not the polish.

Learning curve

Every stream taught me something: lighting, audio, stable connections, and how to keep the action on screen. The community feedback fueled the next experiments.

  • Iterate after every game.
  • Document what worked.

Why I keep doing it

It is rewarding to help families share highlights and to see kids get excited about rewatching their shifts. The tech is fun, but the people matter most.

  • Celebrate growth and effort.
  • Keep the focus on the players.

What I hope you see

You do not need a huge budget or a media company to share meaningful moments. Anyone can build something thoughtful with the tools we already have.

  • Start small and learn fast.
  • Make it your own.
