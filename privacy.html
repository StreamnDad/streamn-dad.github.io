Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to Streamn Dad (StreaMN Dad) and its associated channels and pages.

Overview

Streamn Dad is a personal project operated by Jake Remitz in Minnesota, United States. The goal is to share youth hockey livestreams and highlights and to demonstrate what everyday technology can enable.

Information We Collect

This website does not collect personal information through forms, logins, or analytics. Any information associated with livestreams or social posts is publicly available on the platforms where it appears. Streamn Dad does not use third-party analytics, trackers, or advertising pixels.

How Information Is Used

Publicly available information (such as videos, descriptions, or public comments) may be used only to share, organize, or improve livestreams and highlight content for the community. No private data is sold or shared.
