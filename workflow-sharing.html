Workflow & Sharing

The best workflows are fast, repeatable, and easy for families to follow.

Record while streaming

Capture a local file so highlights are ready right away.

  • Keep a backup of every game.
  • Clip the best moments later.

Quick highlights

Short clips are easiest to share with busy families.

  • Pull goals and key plays.
  • Keep clips under a minute.

Recaps and reels

Bundle the story of the game into a few minutes.

  • Start with a strong moment.
  • End with a team highlight.

Distribution

Publish where families already spend time.

  • YouTube for full games.
  • Shorts and reels for highlights.

Consistency

Keep a predictable cadence so people know when to look.

  • Share a link before puck drop.
  • Post highlights within 24 hours.
