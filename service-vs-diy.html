Service vs DIY

There is no single right path. Choose the option that fits your time, budget, and comfort level, then iterate from there.

Streaming service

A platform handles hosting, encoding, and distribution.

  • Fast setup with fewer moving parts.
  • Less control over overlays and workflow.

DIY setup

You control the tools, quality, and presentation.

  • More flexibility for graphics and updates.
  • Requires more testing and prep.

How to choose

Ask what is most important for your first season.

  • Speed and simplicity vs customization.
  • Budget and time available each week.

Hybrid approach

Start with a service, then add DIY tools as you grow.

  • Use overlays or scoreboards later.
  • Keep the learning curve manageable.
